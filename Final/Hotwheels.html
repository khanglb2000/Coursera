About Hotwheels

Hot Wheels is a brand of die-cast toy cars introduced by American toy maker Mattel in 1968. ... Although Hot Wheels were originally intended for children and young adults, they have become popular with adult collectors, for whom limited edition models are now made available.



Why I love Hotwheels ?

  1. I collect them because of my interests in cars.
  2. Hot wheels manufacture cars which are scale models of many original cars.
  3. It is not so expensive. The price of this die-cast toys has not changed much for over 50 years.
